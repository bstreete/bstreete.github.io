## Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 8 to 10 pound ham (pre-cooked)
| 3 cloves garlic
| 3 jalapeño peppers
| barbeque sauce
| 6 green onions
|
|===

## Directions
. Sliver the onion, garlic, and jalapeños. 
. With a sharp knife, make a deep X into the ham (6 times, space the holes evenly). 
. In each hole, stuff one piece of green onion, 1/2 of a garlic clove, and 1/2 of a jalapeño. 
. Pour barbeque sauce over ham, leaving roughly 1 in the bottom of the pan. 
. Place in oven at 400° or over low barbeque grill. Cook for 1 1/2 hours.
