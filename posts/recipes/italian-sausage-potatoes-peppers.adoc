## Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 1 1/2 pounds Italian sausage
| 3 to 5 Russet potatoes
| 3 green peppers
| 1/4 cup water
| Salt and pepper to taste
| 1 onion

|===

## Directions
. Preheat oven to 400Â°.
. Slice potatoes thin and layer on bottom of baking dish. Season potatoes. 
. Thinly slice peppers and onions; layer over the potatoes before seasoning.
. Crumble uncooked sausage over the vegetables. 
. Pour water into baking dish; cover dish with foil. 
. Bake 1 1/2 hours. Remove foil and bake for an additional hour. 

Serve with garlic bread and salad. Serves 6.
