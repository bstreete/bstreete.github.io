## Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 8 oz feta cheese, crumbled
| 4 oz cream cheese, softened
| 1 clove garlic, minced
| 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
| 1/4 c chopped olives
| 2 tbsp sliced green onions
| 1/2 c chopped walnuts

|===

## Directions
. Mix feta cheese, cream cheese, and oil with electric mixer on medium speed until well blended. Add olives and onions, mix well.
. Toast walnuts in a large non-stick skillet on medium-high heat 5 minutes, stirring constantly, cool.
. Shape feta cheese mixture into a log, roll in walnuts. Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate.
. Remove plastic wrap, serve with assorted crackers.
