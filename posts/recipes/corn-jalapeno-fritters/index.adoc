## Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 2 beaten large eggs
| 1/4 c all purpose flour
| 2 tbsp grated parmesan
| 1/2 tsp kosher salt
| 2 c fresh corn kernels
| 1 thinly sliced scallion
| 1/2 finely chopped jalapeno
| 2 tbsp vegetable oil
| Sour cream (optional)
| Lime wedges (optional)

|===

## Directions

. Pulse eggs, flour, parmesan, and kosher salt in a food processor to combine. Add corn, scallion, and jalapeno. Pulse 2 to 3 times.
. Heat vegetable oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Working in batches, cook heaping tablespoonfuls of batter until golden brown, about 4 minutes per side. Season fritters with salt.
. Serve with sour cream and lime wedges.
