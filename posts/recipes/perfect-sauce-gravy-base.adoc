## Ingredients

### Base Ingredients
[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 3 tablespoons of fat (ex: butter or grease)
| 3 tablespoons of flour
| 2 cups liquid (ex: stock or milk)
|
|=== 

#### Variations

Sauce:: Add green chile
Gravy:: Use stock for the liquid
Gravy for Biscuits::  Use bacon grease for fat and milk for liquid

## Directions
. In a sauce pan, heat the fat. 
. Whisk in flour until well combined and no unincorporated flour remains. Brown for two minutes. 
. Slowly add liquid while stirring constantly. 
. Stirring constantly, bring to a simmer and cook until desired consistency.
