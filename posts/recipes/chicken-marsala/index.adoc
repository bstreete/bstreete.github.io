## Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 1/4 cup flour
| 1/2 teaspoon salt
| 1/4 teaspoon pepper
| 1/2 teaspoon oregano
| 4 tablespoons butter
| 1 cup sliced mushrooms
| 1 1/2 cup marsala wine
| 3/4 cup cooking sherry
| 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts pounded to 1/4 thick
|
|===

## Directions
. In a small bowl, combine flour, salt, pepper, and oregano. Coat chicken pieces in mixture and set aside. 
. In large skillet, melt butter in oil over medium heat. Cook chicken until lightly brown. 
. Add mushrooms, sherry, and marsala. 
. Cover skillet and simmer until checken is fully cooked. 
. Thicken sauce with water/cornstarch
