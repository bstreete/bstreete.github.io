## Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 2 pounds ground beef
| 1/2 cup chopped onion
| 1 jar spaghetti sauce
| salt and pepper
| 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
| 8 oz cream cheese
| 4 cups egg noodles
| 1 cup cottage cheese
| 1/4 cup sour cream
| 1/3 cup green onions
| 1/4 cup green pepper
| Parmesan cheese
| Mozzerella cheese
|
|===

Serve with garlic bread and salad. 

## Directions
. Brown ground beef with onion and garlic. Stir with spaghetti sauce; turn to low. 
. Cook noodles. While noodles are cooking, preheat oven to 350Â°. 
. In a separate bowl, combine softened cream cheese, cottage cheese, sour cream, green peppers, and green onions. 
. In a baking dish, spread half of the noodles over the bottom. Top the noodles with half of the meat sauce. Top with half of the cheese mixture. Sprinkle with mozzerella. 
. Repeat the layers. Sprinkle mozzerella and parmesan over the top. 
. Bake for 45 minutes.
