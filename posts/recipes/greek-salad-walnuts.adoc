## Ingredients

### Salad
[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 1 package (10 oz) mixed salad greens
| 1 c ripe olives
| 3 plum tomatoes cut into wedges
| 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
| 1/2 medium cucumber, peeled, cut into wedges
| 1/2 c coarsely chopped walnuts
| 8 oz feta cheese
|
|===

### Dressing
[cols="<,<"]
|===

| 1/2 c Extra Virgin Olive Oil
| 1/4 c lemon juice
| 1 clove garlic, minced
| 1 tsp dried oregano leaves
| 1/4 tsp pepper
| 1/4 tsp salt

|===

## Directions
. Mix salad dressings together.
. Toss greens, olives, tomatoes, onion, and cucumber with dressing. Spoon into serving plate.
. Toast walnuts in a large non-stick skillet on medium-high heat for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
. Arrange cheese over salad; sprinkle with walnuts.
