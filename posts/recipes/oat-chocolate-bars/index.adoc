#### Notes
Can double recipe and use a 13 by 9 pan. Do not attempt to do more than double or the end result is soggy. 

## Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 1/2 cup flour
| 1/2 teaspoon salt
| 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
| 1 1/4 cup quick oats
| 1 6 oz package of chocalate chips
| 1/2 cup shortening or margarine
| 1 cup brown sugar
| 1 teaspoon vanilla
| 2 eggs

|===

## Directions
. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and quick oats. 
. Cream shortening with brown sugar. Add vanilla and eggs; beat well. 
. Add dry ingredients, then chocolate chips. Bake at 350Â° for 35 minutes.
