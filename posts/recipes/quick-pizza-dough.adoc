## Ingredients 
[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 2 cups all-purpose flour (or 1:1 whole wheat)
| 1 tsp kosher salt
| 1 tbsp instant yeast
| 3 tbsp olive oil
| 1 cup hot tap water
|===

## Directions

. Mix the dry & wet ingredients separately, then combine. 
Knead in the food processor until it holds into a ball (adding more flour through the feed tube). By hand, knead in ~1/4 cup more flour ~5 minutes. 
. Turn the bowl over the dough (upside down) and let rise 15 minutes (prep your toppings now). 
. Stretch, top and bake 425F ~20 minutes. 
Makes 2 crusts.
