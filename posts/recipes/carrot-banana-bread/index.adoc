#### Notes
* Makes 1 9 by 5 loaf pan. 
* Can double recipe and put in a bundt pan to serve 10. 
* Lis always skips the topping.

## Ingredients

### Bread Ingredients
[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 8 oz flour
| 1 teaspoon baking soda
| 1/2 teaspoon salt
| 5 oz brown sugar
| 4 oz grated carrots
| 2 ripe bananas mashed
| 2 eggs
| 1/2 cup vegetable oil
|===

### Optional Topping Ingredients
[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 2 oz soft margarine or cream cheese
| 6 oz sugar
| grated rind and juice of 1 orange
| 1/2 egg white beaten
| orange wedges to decorate
|
|===

## Directions
. Preheat oven to 350Â°. 
. Mix the flour, baking powder, soda, and salt together. Can sift ingredients if so inclined. 
. Stir in brown sugar, carrot, and bananas. 
. Mix lightly, then blend the eggs and oil into the mixture. Pour into floured and greased pan. 
. Bake for 1 hour to 1 hour 15 minutes until toothpick comes out clean. 
. For topping, beat all ingredients together (except orange wedges), until light and fluffy. Then, spread over cake. Decorate with wedges.
