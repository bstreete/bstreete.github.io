## Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===

| 1/2 cup butter
| 1 cup sugar
| 2 beaten eggs
| 3 ripe, mashed bananas
| 2 cups all purpose flour
| 1 teaspoon salt
| 1 teaspoon baking soda mixed with 3 tablespoons of cold water
| 1 teaspoon vanilla
| 1/2 cup chopped nuts
|===

## Directions
. Cream butter and sugar until light. 
. Add beaten eggs and mashed bananas.
. Sift flour and salt together and add to the first mixture. 
. Add baking soda, vanilla, and nuts and mix well. 
. Bake for 1 hour to 1 hour 15 minutes at 325­°. 

### Notes
Makes one 9" by 5" loaf.
