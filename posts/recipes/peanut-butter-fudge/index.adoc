## Ingredients 

[cols="<,<"]
|===
| ½ cup butter
| 2 cups package brown sugar
| ½ cup milk
| 1 cup peanut butter
| 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
| 3 cups confectioners' sugar
|===

Note: modified the sugar and peanut butter amounts compared to the original recipe. 

## Directions

. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat
Stir in brown sugar and milk. 
Bring to a boil and boil for 2 minutes, stirring frequently. 
. Remove the pot from heat; stir in peanut butter and vanilla. 
. Place confectioners' sugar in a large mixing bowl. 
Pour in peanut butter mixture and beat with an electric mixer until smooth. 
. Pour peanut butter mixture into an 8x8-inch dish. 
. Chill until firm before slicing, about 1 hour.
