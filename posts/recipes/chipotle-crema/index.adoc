## Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 1/2 cup low fat Greek yogurt
| 1 tablespoon of sauce from chipotle chili peppers in adobo
| 1 teaspoon lime juice
| 1/2 teaspoon honey
| pinch of salt

|===

## Directions
. Wash hands with soap and warm water.
. Place all ingredients in a small bowl and stir until smooth. 
. Taste and add more lime, chipotle, or honey if needed.
. Serve cold on sandwiches, wraps, or hamburgers. Keeps for up to one week in a sealed container in the refrigerator. 

Note: For a stronger flavor, place 1-2 of the chipotle chiles in a blender with recipe ingredients and blend until smooth.
