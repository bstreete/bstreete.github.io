## Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 2 tablespoons flour
| 1 teaspoon basil
| 1 tablespoon onion powder
| 4 teaspoons chili powder
| 2 teaspoons cumin
| 1 tablespoon cayenne
| 1 tablespoon garlic powder
| 2 teaspoons sugar
| 3 to 4 cloves of garlic
| 1 onion
| 1 bell pepper
| 1 1/2 pounds of ground beef
| 1 15oz can of tomato sauce
| 1 15oz can of diced tomatoes
| 1 15oz can of corn
| 1 15oz can of kidney beans
| 1 can of beer (optional)
|===

## Directions

. Brown the beef in a skillet. 
. Drain cans of corn and beans. 
Combine contents of all cans into pot. 
Bring pot to a boil, then reduce heat to simmer. 
Add meat when finished browning. 
. Saute garlic, onion, and pepper in skillet. 
Add vegetables to pot. 
. Add remaining ingredients to pot and mix. 
. Cook for a few hours (longer cooking improves flavor). 
Can cook in pressure cooker at about 30 minutes at pressure. 
. Serve with crackers and cheese. 

### Tips 

Can cook the spices in the grease left from browning the ground beef before cooking the vegetables for a richer flavor.
