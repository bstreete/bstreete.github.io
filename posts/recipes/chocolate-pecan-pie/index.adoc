## Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 1 15 oz premade pie crust
| 3 eggs, slightly beaten
| 1 cup karo light or dark corn syrup
| 1/2 cup sugar
| 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips, melted
| 2 tablespoons margarine, melted
| 1 teaspoon vanilla
| 1 1/2 cup pecans

|===

## Directions
. Prepare pie crust according to package directions. Flute as directed. 
. In medium bowl, stir eggs, corn syrup, sugar, chocolate, margarine, and vanilla until well blended. 
. Stir in pecans. Pour into the pie crust lined pan. 
. Bake in 350Â° oven for 50 to 60 minutes or until knife inserted halfway between center and edge comes out clean. Cool on wire rack.
