## Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 1 cup flour
| 1 cup milk
| 1/4 cup sugar (for dinner crepes (ex: ham & cheese), decrease sugar by 1/2)
| 4 eggs
| 1 tablespoon oil
| 
|===

## Directions
. Mix flour, milk, sugar, eggs, and oil in bowl. Batter should be runny.
. Heat medium size nonstick frying pan to medium heat.
. Spray pan with cooking spray (only on first crepe).
. Place 1/4 cup batter in pan an switch around until bottom is covered. Cook on both sides like pancakes.

Serves four people.
