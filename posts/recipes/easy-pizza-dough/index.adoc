## Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===

| 1 c warm water
| 1 package of yeast
| 1 tsp salt
| 2 1/2 c of flour
| 2 Tbsp olive oil
| 
|===

## Directions

. Dissolve yeast in warm water
. Mix in flour, salt, and oil in 20 hard strokes. (Don't overmix)
. Let rest 20 minutes.
. Flour your hands, and divide into two portions.
. On an oiled and cornmealed pan, pat the dough into an ~ 11 in. circle using your hands.
. Bake at 425 for 10 minutes.
. Remove from oven and add toppings. Return to oven for another 10 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
