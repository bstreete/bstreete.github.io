## Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 1/4 c olive oil
| 2 tbsp sherry vinegar
| salt
| pepper
| 1 sliced small shallot
| 1/2 c torn fresh herbs (like dill, mint, and/or - chives)
| 3 c raw or blanched corn kernels

|===

## Directions
. Whisk olive oil and vinegar in a large bowl. Season with salt and pepper, add the shallot and herbs.
. Fold in the corn kernels, and season with salt and pepper.
