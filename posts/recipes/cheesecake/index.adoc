## Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===
|   1 graham (or other) crust
|   2 pkg cream cheese
|   1/3 c sugar
|   1 tbls milk
|   1 tbls vanilla
|   1 egg

|===

## Directions
. Mix everything but the crust together. Pour the mix into the crust.
. Bake in a preheated oven at 350 for 30 minutes.
. Let cool and enjoy.
