## Ingredients

### Crust Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 1 1/4 cup graham cracker crumbs
| 1/4 cup soft margarine (or butter)
| 2 tablespoons sugar
|
|===

### Filling Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 12 oz cream cheese
| 1/2 cup sugar
| 2 eggs, well beaten
| 1/4 teaspoon rum extract
| 2 cup fresh or frozen blueberries
|
|===

### Topping Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 1 cup sour cream
| 2 tablespoons sugar
| 2 tablespoons sweet sherry
|
|===

## Directions
. Preheat oven to 350Â°. 
. In bowl, mix crumbs, butter, and sugar. Press mixture into the bottom and sides of an ungreassed 9 pie plate. 
. In bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy. Gradually beat in sugar. 
. Beat in eggs and rum extract. Fold in blueberries. Pour mixture into graham cracker crust. 
. Bake 25 minutes or until filling is set. Remove from oven and cool on rack. 
. Mix topping ingredients and spread over the pie. Garnish with additional fresh blueberries. Chill until serving time.
