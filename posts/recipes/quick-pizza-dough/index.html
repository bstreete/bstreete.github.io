Quick Pizza Dough

2025-12-11

Source

Original site

Suzanne Lenzer

Tags:

  • pizza

Ingredients

2 cups all-purpose flour (or 1:1 whole wheat)

1 tsp kosher salt

1 tbsp instant yeast

3 tbsp olive oil

Directions

  1. Mix the dry & wet ingredients separately, then combine. Knead in the food processor until it holds into a ball (adding more flour through the feed tube). By hand, knead in ~1/4 cup more flour ~5 minutes.

  2. Turn the bowl over the dough (upside down) and let rise 15 minutes (prep your toppings now).

  3. Stretch, top and bake 425F ~20 minutes. Makes 2 crusts.
