## Ingredients

[cols="<,<"]
|===
| 1/2 lb stemmed Fresno chiles (8 - 10)
| 6 peeled garlic cloves
| 3 tbsp red wine vinegar
| Salt, to taste

|===

## Directions
. Cook chiles and garlic in large saucepan of boiling salted water until chiles turn red, about 2 minutes. Drain.
. Blend chiles, garlic, and vinegar in a blender until almost smooth. Season with salt and let cool.
. Can be made a week ahead. Cover and chill.
