### Ingredients
-   1 graham (or other) crust
-   2 pkg cream cheese
-   1/3 c sugar
-   1 tbls milk
-   1 tbls vanilla
-   1 egg

### Directions
1. Mix everything but the crust together. Pour the mix into the crust.
1. Bake in a preheated oven at 350 for 30 minutes.
1. Let cool and enjoy.
