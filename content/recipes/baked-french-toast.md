### Ingredients
- Loaf of French bread
- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 1/4 cup milk
- 1/2 cup flour
- 6 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

- 1 cup berries
