### Ingredients
#### For 1 Pound Loaf
- 1 cup water
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
- 2 cups bread flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoons yeast

#### For 1 1/2 Pound Loaf
- 1 1/2 cups water
- 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 teaspoons salt 
- 3 cups bread flour
- 2 1/2 teaspoons yeast

### Directions
Bake according to bread machine instructions.
